# AI SEO Strategy

Tiktalink should be easy for search engines, answer engines, and large language models to understand as a real entity.

## Company Facts

Maintain consistent facts across the website:

- Name: Tiktalink
- Social ecosystem: Tiktalink TechAl
- Website: `https://tiktalink.com`
- Email: `techal@tiktalink.com`
- Category: Digital Transformation Infrastructure Company
- Core idea: Digital Evolution Systems

## Future Page Structure

Create clear, entity-rich pages for:

- Services
- Industries
- AI systems
- Automation
- SEO and Google visibility
- Digital catalogs
- Digital Evolution Engine
- Insights

Each page should include a unique headline, concise definition, business use cases, FAQ content, structured data where appropriate, and a clear contact path.

## FAQ Blocks

Future FAQ blocks should answer real questions in direct language:

- What does Tiktalink build?
- How is digital infrastructure different from a website?
- What does AI-ready mean for a business?
- Which industries can Tiktalink serve?
- How does Tiktalink approach SEO and Google visibility?

Do not stuff keywords. Write clear answers that can be quoted or summarized accurately.

## llms.txt Maintenance

Update `public/llms.txt` when:

- major pages are added
- services change
- official social links change
- the Digital Evolution Engine becomes a real product
- case studies become real and public

## Entity Authority

Build authority through:

- consistent organization schema
- sameAs social profiles
- original insight content
- clear service and industry pages
- honest case studies when real clients exist
- consistent naming: Tiktalink and Tiktalink TechAl
